About

Both neuroscience and AI study how intelligent systems perceive, make decisions, communicate, and act in the world. Despite rapid developments in both fields, critical questions remain at their intersection: How can AI enhance neuroscientific inquiry, and how can neuroscience insights advance AI development? Our proposed focus group aims to bridge this gap by bringing together researchers to address these interdisciplinary challenges.
